Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with Meliora Designs, including via WhatsApp and our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide (e.g., name, phone number, email address, shipping address, payment info).
  • Messages and communications sent through WhatsApp or our website.
  • Device or usage information (e.g., IP address, browser type).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill orders.
  • To communicate with you about your orders, products, services, or promotions.
  • To provide customer support via WhatsApp, email, or phone.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share your data with service providers (e.g., payment processors, shipping companies) only as needed to fulfill orders.
  • We may disclose your info if required by law (e.g., for legal requests or fraud prevention).

4. WhatsApp Use

  • We use WhatsApp to confirm orders, provide updates, answer questions, and send promotional messages only with your consent.
  • You can opt out of WhatsApp messages at any time by replying with "STOP" or contacting us.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as required for operational or legal purposes.

7. Your Rights

You may access, update, or request deletion of personal data by contacting us at contact@melioradesigns.online.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; changes will be posted on our website.
