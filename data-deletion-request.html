How to Request Deletion of Your User Data

We respect your right to privacy and allow you to request that your personal data be deleted from our records at any time.

  1. Email Request:

    Send an email to contact@melioradesigns.online from the address associated with your account, requesting deletion of your data. Please include your full name and any relevant information to help us identify your account.

  2. WhatsApp Request:

    You may also request data deletion via WhatsApp by messaging our business account with the text "Request Data Deletion". We will reply with further instructions and verify your identity before proceeding.

  3. Through the App/Website:

    If you are logged in, navigate to your profile/account settings and select ‘Delete My Data’. Follow the prompts to confirm your request.

Processing Time: Data deletion requests will be addressed within 30 days. You will receive confirmation once your data has been erased. If data cannot be deleted for legal reasons, we will inform you with details.
